Instagram Follower Analysis Tool

Discover who's following you back with our simple and secure Instagram follower analysis tool. Analyze your followers and following lists to manage your Instagram connections effectively.

1

Access Your Instagram Data

  • Go to your Profile page on Instagram.
  • Settings > Accounts Center > Your Information and Permissions > Download Your Information.
  • Click on the Download or Transfer Your Information button.
  • Select Some of Your Information option.
  • Check Followers and Following from the list and click Next.
  • Click the Download to Device button.
  • Select All Time from the date range.
  • Click the Create Files button.
2

Download the Files

  • Wait for the files to be created (this may take a few minutes).
  • You'll see an Download button on the Download Your Information screen.
  • Click the Download button to save the ZIP file to your computer.
3

Prepare the Files

  • Extract the downloaded ZIP file.
  • Locate the followers_1.html and following.html files.
4

Analyze Your Data

  • Drag the followers_1.html file to the "Followers File" area.
  • Drag the following.html file to the "Following File" area.
  • Click the Analyze Data button.
  • View your results in different tabs:
    • Not Following Back: Users who don't follow you back.
    • Not Following: Users you don't follow.
    • Followers: All your followers.
    • Following: All users you follow.
